Booking Information

At BellaRoux Crafts, we want your paint party experience to be smooth and enjoyable! To ensure everything runs perfectly, paint parties must be booked at least two weeks in advance to allow us to gather the required supplies.

We require a 50% non-refundable deposit upon booking, which covers the cost of these supplies. Please note, the deposit is non-refundable to account for supply costs.

BellaRoux Crafts does not provide the venue or alcohol. If you wish to serve alcohol or food, you will need to arrange that separately.

Taxes and Fees

All final totals will include local and state sales taxes. Additionally, a 3% processing fee will be applied to cover the cost of handling online payments.
